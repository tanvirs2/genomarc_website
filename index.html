GenomeArc Inc.

GenomeArc is a leading analytic platform for high precision interpretation of genomes. Integrating artificial intelligence along with genomic and clinical data for meaningful interpretation of genetic variants.

GenomeArc is committed to resolve genetic determinants for millions of patients with genetic diseases. We want to impact on human health by implementing genomic medicine.
